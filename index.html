Featured Work
Exploring Control Signals

In collaboration with Nicholas Meade, Dr. Scott C. Lowe, and Dr. Oore, I coauthored Exploring Conditioning for Generative Music Systems with Human-Interpretable Controls (ICCC 2019). This work introduced various control signals to the preexisting Performance RNN model. When generating piano performances from a trained model, control signals are a means of influencing the sample that is generated. For example, we introduced a control signal that can be used to mimic the style of a given composer. Using this control signal it is possible to conditionally generate a piano performance on Ravel or Schumann to name just two composers.
